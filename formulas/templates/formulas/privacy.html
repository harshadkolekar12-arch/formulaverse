🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Welcome to FormulaVerse (formulaverse.in). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. We believe in keeping things simple and transparent.
1

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use FormulaVerse. The following data may be collected automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Approximate location (country/city level)
  • Referring website
2

Cookies

FormulaVerse uses cookies to:

  • Save your formula bookmarks (session cookies)
  • Analyze site traffic via Google Analytics
  • Serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

3

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. It collects anonymous usage data. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

5

Third-Party Links

FormulaVerse may contain links to external websites (GitHub, LinkedIn, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

6

Children's Privacy

FormulaVerse is designed for students and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the About page.
